  • About us

To believe in sustainable development and to address this requirement, Solar Umpire has embarked on a strategic plan to build its business in solar energy. We are dedicated to help others take control of their energy costs by suggesting them switch to solar. We believe that shifting to sustainable energy has become imperative in wake of ever increasing global energy needs. By installing solar on their own home, they have reaped the many benefits of solar and want other families to benefit from solar too, all while helping to preserve our beautiful nature.

Our cut above team of developers, project managers, designers and engineers together with top notch knowledge in construction, civil, and electrical engineering offer end to end solution of a solar plant, from site identification and procurement of permits to engineering, procurement and construction (EPC), right through to operation and maintenance (O&M).

We exists to happily serve our community with the incredible experience of benefiting from the power of the sun at their homes or businesses. Since our business is built on happy customers who are eager to refer their friends and family, it’s in our best interest to provide outstanding customer satisfaction and service in every aspect of installation. We want you to be happy and enjoy saving money! We are dedicated to making sure that quality is built into everything we do and that our customers always receive the best products and service, keeping them informed every step of the way.

We are also engaged in distribution of Solar based LED Lighting solutions like Street lights, Solar Home Lights, Solar Lanterns with the support of our strong Technical team with engineering background.

We are working closely with our associates to explore areas for expanding our distribution base of Solar Products.
